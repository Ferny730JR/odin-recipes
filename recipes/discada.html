Discada

Description

Discada is a very yummy Mexican dish. It is a mixed-meat dish cooked on a large "disco" plate. It includes several meats, vegetables, and seasonigs, all mixed together into a scrumptious meal. It is typically prepared outdoors for gatherings and celebrations, and is then served on corn tortillas garnished with cilantro, guacamole, and lime.

Ingredients

  • Bottom Round Roast
  • 4 Winnies
  • Whole White Onion
  • 3 Roma Tomatos
  • 2 Fresh Jalapeños
  • 2 Fresh Jalapeños
